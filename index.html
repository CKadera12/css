Our Service Portfolio

This is the "bread and butter" of our company. Because semiconductor equipment is so sensitive, maintenance is far more rigorous than in typical manufacturing.

Preventive Maintenance (PM): Scheduled servicing to replace consumables (like filters or seals) before they fail.

Corrective Maintenance: Rapid response "break-fix" services to minimize MTTR (Mean Time To Repair) when a machine goes offline.

On-site Engineering: We station full-time field service engineers (FSEs) directly inside the customer's fab to provide 24/7 support.

When a fab buys a new piece of equipment—which can cost upwards of $100 million for EUV (Extreme Ultraviolet) lithography tools—we manage the entire setup process.

Rigging and Placement: Moving massive, delicate tools into cleanroom environments.

Hook-up Services: Connecting the tool to ultra-high-purity gases, chemicals, water, and power.

Calibration: Fine-tuning the hardware to ensure it meets the nanometer-scale precision required for the specific "recipe" the fab is running.

We ensure that the right part is available at the right time, often managing the logistics so the end user doesn't have to.

Spare Parts Kits: Managing local inventories of high-wear components.

Parts Refurbishment: Cleaning and repairing expensive components (like ceramic heaters or electrostatic chucks) so they can be reused rather than replaced.

Obsolescence Management: Finding workarounds or replacements for parts of older machines that are no longer manufactured.

Semiconductor technology moves fast. Instead of buying a new machine every three years, fabs often pay service providers to upgrade their existing fleet.

Throughput Enhancements: Software or hardware tweaks that allow a machine to process more wafers per hour.

Reliability Upgrades: Replacing older, prone-to-failure sub-systems with modern, more robust versions.

Relocation Services: Decontaminating, crating, and moving tools if a company decides to shift production to a different global facility.

We don't just fix hardware; we help with the "science" of making chips.

Yield Enhancement: Analyzing data to figure out why certain wafers have defects and adjusting the equipment to fix it.

Remote Diagnostics: Using IoT sensors and "digital twins" to monitor machine health from across the globe and predict failures before they happen.

Training: Educating the fab's internal technicians on how to operate and maintain the tools safely.
