Our Service Portfolio

This is the "bread and butter" of our company. Because semiconductor equipment is so sensitive, maintenance is far more rigorous than in typical manufacturing.

Preventive Maintenance (PM): Scheduled servicing to replace consumables (like filters or seals) before they fail.

We move, install, and commission complex tools with a precision that meets fab requirements — from crating and cleanroom prep through hook-up, qualification, and first-wafer acceptance.

We source, refurbish, and stock critical spare parts — OEM and equivalent — and keep your consumable pipeline healthy so that scheduled maintenance never waits on logistics.

Extend the life of legacy tools. We design and install hardware, software, and control-system retrofits that boost throughput, yield, and uptime without a full capital replacement.

Data-driven tuning of recipes, chamber conditions, and preventive schedules. Our engineers work alongside your process teams to raise mean-time-between-failure and lower cost-per-wafer.
